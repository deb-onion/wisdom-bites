Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about contacting us

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly at +1 (555) 123-4567 for immediate assistance.

Yes, you can request an appointment through our contact form. However, for the fastest appointment scheduling, we recommend using our online booking system or calling our office directly. The online system shows real-time availability and allows you to confirm your appointment instantly.

We offer free parking for patients in our dedicated lot behind our building. Enter from Dental Avenue and follow the signs for "Patient Parking." If our lot is full, there is a public parking garage one block east on Smile Street with validation available for our patients.

Yes, our office is fully wheelchair accessible. We have ramp access at the main entrance, wheelchair-accessible restrooms, and our treatment rooms are designed to accommodate wheelchairs comfortably. If you require any additional assistance, please let us know when scheduling your appointment.

For dental emergencies outside of our regular office hours, please call our emergency line at +1 (555) 987-6543. This will connect you with our on-call dentist who can provide guidance. For severe emergencies involving significant trauma, uncontrolled bleeding, or severe swelling, please go to your nearest emergency room or call 911.
