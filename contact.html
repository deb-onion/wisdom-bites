Yes, we understand that many of our patients have busy schedules. We offer extended hours on Tuesdays and Thursdays until 7:00 PM, and we're open on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM to accommodate patients who cannot visit during regular weekday hours. Please note that Saturday appointments tend to fill quickly, so we recommend booking these appointments well in advance.

If you experience a dental emergency during office hours, please call us immediately at (555) 123-4567, and we'll do our best to see you the same day. For emergencies outside of regular hours, call our emergency line, which will connect you with the on-call dentist.

For specific emergency situations:

  • Knocked-out tooth: Retrieve the tooth by the crown (not the root), rinse gently with water, and try to reinsert it into the socket. If that's not possible, keep it in milk or saliva and seek immediate dental care.
  • Severe pain or swelling: Take over-the-counter pain medication (as directed on the packaging), apply a cold compress, and contact us immediately.
  • Broken tooth: Rinse your mouth with warm water, apply a cold compress to reduce swelling, and call our office.
