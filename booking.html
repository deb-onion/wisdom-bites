We Accept Most Insurance Plans

Making quality dental care accessible

At Wisdom Bites Dental Clinic, we work with most major dental insurance providers to help maximize your benefits and minimize out-of-pocket expenses. Our administrative team is happy to verify your coverage and explain your benefits before treatment.

If you don't see your insurance provider listed or have questions about coverage, please contact our office for assistance.

What to Expect at Your First Appointment

Making your visit comfortable and productive

New Patient Forms

Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete new patient forms, or download them from our website and bring them completed.

Medical History Review

We'll review your medical history and any concerns you have about your dental health.

Digital X-Rays

We may take digital x-rays to help diagnose issues not visible during the oral examination.

Comprehensive Examination

Your dentist will perform a thorough examination of your teeth, gums, and oral tissues.

Treatment Discussion

We'll discuss our findings and recommend a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.
