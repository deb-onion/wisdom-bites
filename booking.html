Patient Information

What to Expect at Your Visit

Making your dental experience comfortable and stress-free

Before Your Visit

Download and complete our new patient forms ahead of time to save time during your appointment. Please arrive 15 minutes early if you need to complete forms in our office.

During Your Visit

We'll review your medical history, take necessary X-rays, and perform a comprehensive examination. Your dentist will discuss their findings and recommend a personalized treatment plan.

Professional Cleaning

Our skilled hygienist will perform a thorough cleaning to remove plaque and tartar, polish your teeth, and provide oral hygiene instructions tailored to your needs.

After Your Visit

We'll schedule any necessary follow-up appointments and send you home with care instructions. You'll also receive a reminder for your next routine check-up.

Have Questions About Your Visit?

Our team is happy to address any concerns you may have before your appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common booking questions

For routine check-ups and cleanings, we recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure you get your preferred day and time. For specific treatments or to see a particular dentist, booking 3-4 weeks ahead is advisable. If you have a dental emergency, please call us immediately at (555) 123-4567, and we'll do our best to see you on the same day.

We understand that schedules change. You can reschedule or cancel your appointment online through our patient portal or by calling our office at (555) 123-4567. We request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or changes to avoid a $50 cancellation fee. For Monday appointments, please notify us by Friday at noon.

Appointment durations vary based on the type of service:

  • New patient exams and cleanings: 60-90 minutes
  • Routine check-ups and cleanings: 45-60 minutes
  • Fillings: 30-60 minutes per tooth
  • Cosmetic consultations: 45 minutes
  • Emergency appointments: Varies based on treatment needed
The estimated duration will be provided when you book your appointment.

Yes, we send appointment confirmations via email immediately after booking. Additionally, you'll receive a reminder email 48 hours before your appointment and a text message reminder (if you provided a mobile number) 24 hours in advance. If you haven't received your confirmation email, please check your spam folder or contact our office to verify your appointment.

For your first visit, please bring:

  • Photo ID (driver's license or other government-issued ID)
  • Dental insurance card (if applicable)
  • Completed new patient forms (download from our website or arrive 15 minutes early to complete them)
  • List of current medications
  • Any recent dental X-rays (if available)
  • Payment method for any co-pays or fees not covered by insurance
